TenKabel @Simple2B Account Deletion

To delete your account, send a DELETE request to the following email: istacat.info@gmail.com

Content of email should be as follows:

{
    "username": your_username,
    "phone": your_phone_number
}

If you have any questions, please contact us at the same email address.

With this delete request, you will lose all your data (including job history, payment information) and will not be able to recover it.
